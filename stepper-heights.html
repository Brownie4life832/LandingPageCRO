The Problem

01

When your legs don't move, blood pools in your lower body. That's why your ankles swell, your feet go numb, and your legs feel heavy by evening.

Without regular motion, joints lose their range. Stiffness becomes pain. Pain becomes inactivity. Inactivity makes the stiffness worse. It's a cycle that accelerates every day.
