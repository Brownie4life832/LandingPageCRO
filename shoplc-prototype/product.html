Tanzanite is found in only one place on earth — a small mining area near Mt. Kilimanjaro in Tanzania. Geologists estimate the supply could be exhausted within the next 20-30 years, making it 1,000 times rarer than diamond. Its trichroic nature means it displays three different colors depending on the viewing angle: blue, violet, and burgundy.
